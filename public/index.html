You Pray ✪

Roll 1d10

  • 1: You gain 1 disorder
  • 2-4: You gain +★ insanity
  • 5-8: You gain +★ to all hit locations
  • 9: You gain +1 luck
  • 10: Gain ✪, if you roll while spending ✪, roll 2d10 and select which result to count

Chimes jingle as the weather shifts

You Dance ✪

Roll 1d10

  • 1: You gain -1 movement
  • 2-4: You gain +1 evasion
  • 5-7: You gain +1 movement
  • 8-10: You and another survivor gain Synchronized Strike secret fighting art

Sturdy packs rest gathered together

You Prepare Your Gear ✪

Roll 1d10

  • 1: You gain -1 survival
  • 2-4: You gain +★ survival
  • 5-7: You gain +1 strength
  • 8-10: All survivors gain +1 survival

Faces worn with footwork mark a place of battle

You Train ✪

Roll 1d10

  • 1: You gain -1 strength
  • 2-4: Draw 2 fighting arts, you gain 1
  • 5-7: You gain +1 accuracy
  • 8-9: You may copy any fighting art or secret fighting art another survivor has
  • 10: Gain an additional weapon specialization, or upgrade a specialization into a mastery

A bench and tools sit idly waiting for hands

You Craft ✪

Choose One:

  • Obtain a copy of a paired weapon already in camp
  • Sacrifice a weapon to obtain another weapon

  • Sacrifice an armour set, obtain another random armour set

  • Sacrifice up to 3 non-armour, non-weapon gear pieces to obtain new ones

A cracked face oozes black saccharine ichor

You Imbibe ✪

Roll 1d10

  • 1: Gain Possessed ability
  • 2: Gain Bitter Frenzy ability
  • 3: Gain Acid Palms ability
  • 4: Gain Peerless ability
  • 5: Gain Leyline Walker ability
  • 6: Gain Nightmare Spurs ability
  • 7: Gain Iron Will ability
  • 8: Gain "Your complete affinities and incomplete affinity halves count as all colours." ability
  • 9-10: Choose your outcome

Roll Hit Location

  • Head: Suffer Deaf
  • Body: Suffer Broken Rib
  • Legs: Suffer Broken Leg
  • Arms: Suffer Broken Arm
  • Waist: Suffer Broken Hip

Rules:

  • If anything during camp causes a survivor to not be able to depart (or die), perform the random action again.
  • All gear in "They Carry" is not assigned until camp phase ends (this includes cursed gear).
  • Specializations and masteries are assigned to specific survivors before departure.
  • Custom Scout gear grids here, however the default grid may also be used. Original idea for 6 player rules from Fen who you can support on Patreon.
  • Follow standard rules for showdowns, including a hunt phase for quarries.
  • Character milestones happen normally, ignore settlement abilities from bold, and insight.
