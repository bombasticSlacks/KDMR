You Pray ✪

You Dance ✪

Roll 1d10

  • 1: You gain -1 movement
  • 2-4: You gain +1 evasion
  • 5-7: You gain +1 movement

You Prepare Your Gear ✪

Roll 1d10

  • 1: You gain -1 survival
  • 2-4: You gain +★ survival
  • 5-7: You gain +1 strength

You Train ✪

Roll 1d10

  • 1: You gain -1 strength

  • 5-7: You gain +1 accuracy
  • 8-10: You may copy any fighting art or secret fighting art another survivor has
